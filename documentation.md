// Spotify Playlist App — Business & User Overview

## What it is
- A small service that keeps a Spotify playlist in sync with the music played on DR P3.
- The playlist is called “P3 (Updated live)”.

## What it does
- Checks DR P3 regularly (every 15 minutes, 05:00–20:59 UTC).
- Adds new songs it finds to your Spotify playlist.
- Avoids duplicates and automatically removes items older than 7 days.
- Keeps the playlist cover and description up to date.

## Who it’s for
- Listeners who want a fresh, auto‑updated P3 music playlist without manual effort.

## What you get
- A living playlist you can follow, share, and play at any time.
- Clean track list (no repeats, only what’s new).

## Using it
- Do nothing: it updates itself on schedule.
- Open Spotify and play “P3 (Updated live)”.
- If you prefer another name, you can create a second playlist with your own title (same behavior).

## Key behavior (plain language)
- New songs only: already‑present tracks are skipped.
- Recent focus: items older than one week are cleared automatically.
- Regular refresh: checks multiple times per hour during the day.

That’s it — open Spotify and enjoy the latest P3 music without lifting a finger.
